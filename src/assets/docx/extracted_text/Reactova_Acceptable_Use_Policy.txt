Acceptable Use Policy
reactova.com — Last updated: April 2026
Effective Date: April 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules for using the Reactova platform. By using Reactova, you agree to comply with this policy. Violation may result in account suspension or termination without refund.

1. Permitted Uses
Automate replies to comments on your own Instagram posts or posts you manage with authorisation
Create bio link pages for your own brand or clients
Track conversion analytics for your own campaigns
Manage multiple Instagram accounts you own or are authorised to manage
Participate in the affiliate program and refer users to the platform

2. Prohibited Activities
2.1 Spam and Unsolicited Messaging
Sending bulk unsolicited DMs to users who have not engaged with your content
Using automation to harass, intimidate, or repeatedly message the same user
Creating fake comments or engagement to trigger automations artificially
Using bots or automated tools to simulate comment activity

2.2 Harmful and Illegal Content
Distributing content that violates intellectual property rights
Sending content promoting violence, hate speech, discrimination, or illegal activities
Distributing adult content or content involving minors
Spreading misinformation or fraudulent claims
Phishing, scamming, or attempting to deceive users into sharing personal information

2.3 Platform Abuse
Attempting to reverse engineer or extract source code from the platform
Performing unauthorised security scanning or penetration testing
Attempting to bypass rate limits, authentication, or access controls
Scraping or harvesting data from the platform through automated means
Creating multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions or bans
Reselling platform access without written permission

2.4 Instagram Policy Violations
Violating Meta's Platform Policies or Instagram's Community Standards
Accessing Instagram accounts you do not own or have authority over
Automating actions that violate Instagram's rate limits or restrictions

2.5 Affiliate Program Abuse
Self-referrals or referring your own accounts for commission
Paying others to sign up through your referral link
Using misleading or deceptive marketing to generate referrals
Creating fake accounts to earn commissions

3. White Label Responsibilities
Agency plan users who white label our platform are responsible for ensuring their clients comply with this policy. You are accountable for your clients' actions on the platform.

4. Consequences of Violation
Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may:
Issue a written warning
Temporarily suspend your account
Permanently terminate your account without refund
Report violations to law enforcement or Meta
Pursue legal action where appropriate

5. Reporting Violations
If you become aware of any user violating this policy, please report it to support@reactova.com. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through the platform. Continued use of the platform after such changes constitutes your acceptance. In some cases, we may require you to explicitly accept updated terms before continuing to use the service.

Material changes include updates that affect your rights, data usage, pricing, or core functionality of the platform.

7. Consistency with Terms
This Acceptable Use Policy should be read alongside our Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Refund Policy. In the event of any conflict between this policy and the Terms and Conditions, the Terms and Conditions shall prevail.

8. Contact
To report violations or for questions: support@reactova.com
