1. What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and deliver a better experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
2.1 Essential Cookies
These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled:
Session authentication cookies — keep you logged in
Security cookies — protect against CSRF and other attacks
Subscription and billing state cookies
Load balancing cookies

2.2 Analytics Cookies
These help us understand how users interact with our platform:
Pages visited and time spent on each page
Features used and workflows created
Error tracking and performance monitoring
Conversion funnel analysis

2.3 Marketing and Tracking Cookies
These help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing:
UTM parameter tracking (source, medium, campaign)
Referral tracking for the affiliate program
Advertising performance measurement

2.4 Preference Cookies
These remember your settings:
Language and region preferences
UI theme and display settings
Recently accessed workspaces

3. Third Party Cookies
Some cookies are set by third party services we use to operate the platform:
Stripe — payment processing and fraud prevention
Razorpay — payment processing for Indian users
Analytics providers — for usage insights
These providers have their own privacy and cookie policies which we recommend you review.

4. Cookie Duration
Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period. Most of our analytics cookies expire within 12 months. Authentication cookies expire at session end or after a period of inactivity.

5. Managing Cookies
You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the platform from functioning correctly. You may also contact us at support@reactova.com to opt out of non-essential cookies.
